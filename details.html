About Place

Garuda Hotel is located near Monumen Nasional (Monas), just a 10-minute walk from the Monas. Built in 1956 it has old school charm and many rooms still feature high, crown-molded ceilings. A 2012 renovation brought all rooms and services up to modern day scratch and guestrooms come equipped with free Wi-Fi and all the usual amenities required for a comfortable stay.
